# Code of Conduct

Moving Stylus is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.
All people are welcome regardless of age, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, disability, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, or background.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or disruptive behavior.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers.
